Le domande — the questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to already speak Italian?

No. Lessons adapt to your level, from complete beginner to advanced. We build a strong foundation — and confidence — from the very first lesson.

Are lessons online or in person?

Both. Learn online from anywhere in the world, or meet Lucia in person in Prague 1, 2 and 3.

How does the free trial work?

It's a relaxed 30-minute session: you meet Lucia, she evaluates your current level, and together you discuss your goals. No obligation to continue.

How do the autumn group courses work?

Each course runs October to December: 12 weekly live lessons in a small group of 3–5 students, at beginner, intermediate or advanced level. You'll get the exact weekly time slot before enrolling.

Can lessons be personalised for my specific goals?

Yes — that's the whole point of private lessons. Business Italian, trip preparation, exam prep: every curriculum is built around your goals.

How do payments work?

Via Revolut or bank transfer. Choose a 5-lesson or 10-lesson private package, or enrol in a group course.
